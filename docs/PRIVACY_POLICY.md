## Introduction

RecallSentry ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using RecallSentry, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Name** (optional)
- **Email address**
- **Password** (encrypted and stored securely)
- **Physical address** (optional, for location-based recalls)

### 2. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- **Device information** (device model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- **FCM device token** (for push notifications)
- **Saved recalls** (recalls you bookmark)
- **Subscription tier information** (Free, SmartFiltering, RecallMatch)
- **App usage analytics** (e.g., features used, time in app)

### 3. Location Data
If you grant permission:
- **Approximate location** (state/region) for state-specific recall filtering
- We do NOT track precise GPS coordinates
- Location permission is optional and can be disabled at any time

### 4. Camera Access
If you grant permission:
- **Camera access** for barcode scanning (SmartScan feature)
- Images are processed locally and NOT stored or transmitted

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Display relevant FDA, USDA, CPSC, and NHTSA recalls
   - Send push notifications about new recalls
   - Filter recalls by your saved preferences (brands, products, states)
   - Provide subscription-based features

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Personalize recall recommendations
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Track subscription limits (e.g., saved recalls, state filters)

3. **Communication**
   - Send important service updates
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Notify you of account changes

4. **Security & Safety**
   - Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
   - Monitor for suspicious activity
   - Enforce our Terms of Service

  - [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy)

### Legal Requirements

---

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **SSL Certificate Pinning:** All API communication is encrypted (HTTPS)
- **Token Encryption:** Auth tokens stored in secure iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
- **Password Hashing:** Passwords are never stored in plain text
- **Session Management:** Automatic logout after 30 days or 4 hours of inactivity
- **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or ad trackers

## Your Rights & Choices

### 1. Account Deletion
You can delete your account at any time:
- **In-App:** Settings → Account → Delete Account
- **Email Us:** [support@recallsentry.com]
- **Data Retention:** All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days

### 2. Data Access & Export
You can request a copy of your data:
- **Email:** [support@recallsentry.com]
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days

### 3. Push Notifications
- **Opt-Out:** Disable in iOS Settings → Notifications → RecallSentry
- **In-App Toggle:** Settings → Notifications

### 4. Location Permission
- **Revoke:** iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → RecallSentry

## Children's Privacy

RecallSentry is NOT intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately at [support@recallsentry.com].

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- **Know:** What personal data we collect and how it's used
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of data sales (Note: We do not sell data)

To exercise these rights, email [support@recallsentry.com].

---

## European Data Protection (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability:** Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, email [support@recallsentry.com].

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active Accounts:** As long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Saved Recalls:** Automatically deleted after 6 months of inactivity
- **Crash Logs:** Retained for 90 days (anonymized)
